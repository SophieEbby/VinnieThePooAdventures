Welcome to Vinnie the Poo Adventures!

This website is your go-to guide for dog owners, packed with recommendations for the best walks, road trips, and helpful tips & tricks in both Australia and the UK. Whether you're exploring local trails or planning a longer adventure, we’re here to help you and your furry friend make the most of every journey!

You might be wondering why this website covers places on opposite sides of the world. I’m Sophie, and I relocated from the UK to Australia with my Cavoodle (or Cavapoo, if you’re in the UK) named Vinnie. This site shares our favorite dog-friendly walks, road trips, and tips to help fellow dog owners enjoy adventures no matter where they are!
