Tips & Tricks

This page is dedicated to sharing useful tips and tricks for dog owners in Australia, specifically the Gold Coast.

Tips

  • Tip 1: Register Your Dog
    In the Gold Coast, it is a legal requirement to register your dog with your local council. Registration helps reunite you with your pet if they get lost and ensures compliance with local regulations. Registration usually needs to be renewed annually, and discounts often apply for desexed dogs or pensioners. You can register online via the Gold Coast City Council website. Register your dog online here.
  • Tip 2: Keep Dogs on a Leash in Public Areas
    Dogs must be on a leash in all public places unless you are in a designated off-leash area. Off-leash areas are specifically marked and safe for your dog to run freely. Check council maps or apps to locate these zones. Always carry a leash and waste bags to clean up after your dog. Check the official list of off-leash areas to find safe zones near you.
  • Tip 3: Consider a Greencross Healthy Pets Plus Subscription
    This is a membership program offered by Greencross Vets that provides unlimited vet consultations, discounted products, and other benefits for a monthly fee. One major advantage is that there are no consultation fees for non-emergency visits, which can save you money over time. Members also get discounts on parasite prevention, vaccinations, and food. Learn about Healthy Pets Plus here.
  • Tip 4: Microchip Your Dog
    Microchipping is mandatory in Queensland. Make sure your contact details are current. Find out about microchipping here.
  • Tip 5: Tick and Flea Prevention
    The Gold Coast is a high-risk area for paralysis ticks. Talk to your vet about the best protection for your dog. Tick prevention tips. Flea prevention tips.

  • Trick 1: Take Advantage of Petbarn’s Price Match Policy
    Petbarn will match the price of any identical product found at a competitor (including online stores). This means you can often get the lowest price without having to shop around at multiple stores. Just show proof of the competitor’s price (e.g., a photo or website link) when making your purchase. See Petbarn's Price Match Policy.
  • Trick 2: Free Poo Bags in Public Places
    Many parks and public areas on the Gold Coast provide free dog waste bags. Look for dispensers at popular dog walking spots such as beaches, off-leash parks, and council-managed reserves. This not only helps you keep the environment clean but also saves you money on buying bags. An on-the-spot fine may apply if you do not dispose of your dog's waste properly in public.
